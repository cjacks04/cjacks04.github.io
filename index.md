I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Wisconsin–Madison [Information School](https://ischool.wisc.edu/).

My research sits at the intersection of human-computer interaction (HCI), computer-supported cooperative work (CSCW), and social computing. I study how people engage with data- and AI-driven systems in public and community settings, with a focus on responsible AI, civic technology, environmental justice, and human–AI collaboration.
